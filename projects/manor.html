MODERN MANOR

PROJECT TYPE:Residential
LOCATION:Hyderabad
BUILT-UP AREA:3540 sq.ft.
SCOPE OF WORK:Interior Design
TIMELINE:2021-2022
TEAM:Srikanth Adepu (Lead PM), Priyanka (Architect)
STAGE:Completed
Ground Floor

Located in Hyderabad, this G+2 villa exemplifies a harmonious blend of modern aesthetics with traditional elements. Designed in collaboration with Ar. Priyanka, the project reflects thoughtful design and seamless execution. Partnering with Muppa Ventures, this villa stands as one of the most iconic projects we've been privileged to undertake, with Srikanth expertly managing execution and client interactions.

First Floor

Design Description: The villa features a sleek modern design with clean lines, open spaces, and functional layouts. To honor the client's cultural preferences, traditional elements were thoughtfully incorporated, adding warmth and timeless charm to the design. The interplay of contemporary materials and traditional motifs creates a unique architectural identity that caters to modern lifestyles while respecting heritage.

Second Floor

Key Highlights:

  • Modern-Traditional Fusion: Innovative integration of traditional elements into a modern framework.
  • Collaborative Excellence: Designed in partnership with Ar. Priyanka, showcasing teamwork and creative synergy.
  • Efficient Management: Seamless execution and client handling by Srikanth ensured timely delivery and quality assurance.
  • Iconic Venture: Part of a prestigious collaboration with Muppa, enhancing the project's scale and prominence.

This project represents a milestone in Novelle Interiors's portfolio, blending design innovation with cultural authenticity. Every corner reflects meticulous planning and artisanal quality.
