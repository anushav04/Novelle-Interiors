Initial Consultation

We start by meeting with the client to understand their vision and preferences. This includes identifying specific needs and style preferences. We then finalize a budget that aligns with their financial plan and project scope.

01

Planning and Design

Our team conducts a detailed site inspection to evaluate the space and any potential challenges. We create a comprehensive design plan, including layout, color schemes, and materials selection, ensuring high-quality choices that fit the client's budget.

02

Execution and Handover

Our skilled team meticulously executes the design plan, focusing on every detail. Upon completion, we conduct a final walkthrough with the client to ensure complete satisfaction before handing over the beautifully finished space.
