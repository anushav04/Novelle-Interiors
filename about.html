Novelle Interiors:
A Legacy of Resilience and Quality

Novelle Interiors is built on a foundation of resilience, dedication, and a passion for quality. Our journey began in the early 2000s with our father, Mr. Lal, who instilled in us the values of hard work through his construction materials business.

Inspired by his perseverance, Mr. Mohammed Ishaq gained expertise in woodwork, while Mr. Mohammed Imran focused on quality control and project execution. Together, they expanded into interior design, laying the foundation for what Novelle Interiors is today.
